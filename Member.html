MEMBERS

Yang Jung-Won

Yang Jung-Won also known by his stage name Jungwon is a South Korean singer, songwriter, dancer, musician, and television personality who is the leader, lead vocalist, and lead dancer of the South Korean boy group Enhypen.

Park Sung-hoon

Park Sung-hoon, known mononymously as Sunghoon, is a South Korean singer and former figure skater. He competed as a figure skater from 2010 to early 2020; while simultaneously being a K-pop trainee since 2018. He retired from the sport and debuted as a member of the South Korean boy band Enhypen in November 2020.

Park Jongseong

Jay is a passionate guitar player. He plays the guitar daily and brings one on tour when he travels. He currently has a collection of 9 guitars. He's good at hip-hop style dance.

Nishimura Riki

Ni-ki is charismatic on stage and playful off stage. He is the maknae who loves to tease and play pranks on his hyungs, but he is also very warm and caring towards his members. His love language to the members is giving his attention and spending time with them.

Lee Heeseung

Heeseung is a perfectionist: “Some people think deeply about one thing, and others think about a lot of things.” ( source) Along with Jungwon, he was considered as one of the candidates to become the leader of ENHYPEN. Heeseung decided to focus on his responsibilities as the eldest of the group.

Sim Jaeyun

Among the members, Jake is the member who laughs the most and gives positive influence. Jungwon said that Jake gives him “positive energy and makes him feel at ease.” Sunoo considers Jake as a member he can go to for advise, and Jake listens to him.

Kim Sunoo

Kim Sunoo was born on 24 June 2003 in Suwon, South Korea. He is an actor, known for Dark Moon: The Blood Altar (2022), Enhypen Untold (2024) and Enhypen: Given-Taken (2020).
